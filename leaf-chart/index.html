Notes

  • This hand knitting pattern is designed to be knit flat.
  • Odd-numbered rows are knit on the RS of the piece, even-numbered rows on the WS.
  • The first row starts on the right hand side of the pattern.

Yarn

MC#FFFFFFWhite

Instructions

Cast on a multiple of 14 stitches plus 8.

  • Row 1 (RS): in MC k3, k2tog; * p2, yo, k1, yo, p2, ssk, k5, k2tog; repeat from * to last 3 stitches; p2, yo, k1.
  • Rows 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14 and 16: in MC p to end of row.
  • Row 3: in MC k2, k2tog; * p2, k1, yo, k1, yo, k1, p2, ssk, k3, k2tog; repeat from * to last 4 stitches; p2, k1, yo, k1.
  • Row 5: in MC k1, k2tog; * p2, k2, yo, k1, yo, k2, p2, ssk, k1, k2tog; repeat from * to last 5 stitches; p2, k2, yo, k1.
  • Row 7: in MC k2tog; * p2, k3, yo, k1, yo, k3, p2, cdd; repeat from * to last 6 stitches; p2, k3, yo, k1.
  • Row 9: in MC k1, yo; * p2, ssk, k5, k2tog, p2, yo, k1, yo; repeat from * to last 7 stitches; p2, ssk, k3.
  • Row 11: in MC k1, yo, k1; * p2, ssk, k3, k2tog, p2, k1, yo, k1, yo, k1; repeat from * to last 6 stitches; p2, ssk, k2.
  • Row 13: in MC k1, yo, k2; * p2, ssk, k1, k2tog, p2, k2, yo, k1, yo, k2; repeat from * to last 5 stitches; p2, ssk, k1.
  • Row 15: in MC k1, yo, k3; * p2, cdd, p2, k3, yo, k1, yo, k3; repeat from * to last 4 stitches; p2, ssk.
