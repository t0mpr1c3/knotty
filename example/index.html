Yarn

MC#FFFFFFwhite
CC1#DDBBFFlavender

Instructions

Cast on a multiple of 8 stitches.

  • Row 1 (RS): * in MC slwyif4, k4; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 2: * in MC p3, slwyif4, p1; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 3: * in MC k2, slwyif4, k2; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 4: * in MC p1, slwyif4, p3; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 5: * in MC k4, slwyif4; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 6: * in MC slwyif3, p4, slwyif1; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 7: * in CC1 k1, slwyif4, k3; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 8: * in CC1 p4, slwyif4; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 9: * in CC1 slwyif3, k4, slwyif1; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 10: * in CC1 slwyif2, p4, slwyif2; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 11: * in CC1 slwyif1, k4, slwyif3; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Row 12: * in CC1 slwyif4, p4; repeat from * to end of row.
  • Repeat rows 112.
